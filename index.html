Chimney Not Absorbing Smoke
Weak or zero suction despite the motor running. Usually caused by blocked filters, a clogged duct, or a worn impeller. We restore full suction in one visit.

Motor Not Starting or Stopped
The chimney powers on but the fan doesn't spin, or it trips the MCB. Motor rewinding, capacitor replacement, or full motor swap — we carry parts.

Loud Noise or Vibration

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Why has my kitchen chimney lost suction power? ▾
Reduced suction is almost always caused by one of three things: a grease-clogged filter, a blocked or undersized duct, or a deteriorating motor. A professional cleaning and inspection resolves most suction issues in a single visit — no part replacement needed. If the motor is genuinely failing, we'll quote you before replacing anything.
Which chimney and hob brands do you service? ▾
We service all major brands sold in India — Faber, Elica, Glen, Hindware, Kutchina, Sunflame, Bosch, Prestige, Pigeon, Inalsa, Kaff, Hafele, Franke, IFB, and more. For hobs we cover all gas and induction brands.
How often should a kitchen chimney be serviced? ▾
For typical Indian cooking with spices and frying, a professional deep clean every 3–6 months is ideal. Lighter use can stretch to 6 months. Regular cleaning prevents grease fires and preserves motor life significantly.
Do you repair gas hobs and induction cooktops too? ▾
Yes. We repair gas hob burner issues (uneven flame, auto-ignition failure, knob damage, gas valve problems) and induction cooktop faults. If your hob came with your chimney, we can service both in one appointment.
Is same-day chimney service available in East Delhi? ▾
Yes, for most East Delhi localities. Call or WhatsApp 7428444313 and we'll check same-day availability for your area. Busy periods may push to next morning but we always give you a clear ETA at the time of booking.
What does the free inspection include? ▾
The technician visits your home, runs the appliance, checks suction levels, inspects the motor, filters, duct, and electrical connections, and identifies the root cause. You receive a written quote before any work begins. The inspection itself is completely free.
